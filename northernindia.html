Northern India


Dalhousie,Himachal Pradesh

"Little Switzerland of India"
Dalhousie is a picturesque hill station, nestled in the Himalayas. One of the most invigorating tourist places in north India, it is characterized by lofty mountains, sparkling river, luxuriant valleys, and colonial houses. Its serenity makes it a perfect choice for honeymooners, solo travelers, and photographers. And while you are here, do not forget to visit the delightful Khajjiar, that is often called the Mini Switzerland of India!
Weather:26 C
Ideal duration:2-3 days
Best time:Throughout the year
Nearest Airport:Jammu


Vaishno Devi,Jammu & Kashmir

"The Holy Caves"
Vaishno Devi is a religious trekking destination where pilgrims walk about 13 km uphill to reach the little caves which are among one of the 108 Shakti Peetha. It takes about 6 to 9 hours depending on the fitness, age and weather conditions. Ponies and palanquins, as well as helicopter services, are available by various vendors to take you there at the top. The whole way is decently paved with lots of shops selling snacks, food items and various religious goods. The pilgrims show their dedication and enthusiasm by shouting slogans and singing songs in the praise of Maa Vaishnavi all along the road.
Weather:28 C
Ideal duration:1 days
Best time:Throughout the year
Nearest Airport:Jammu


Leh Ladakh,Jammu & Kashmir

"India's Own Moonland"
Bounded by two of the world's mightiest mountain ranges, the Great Himalaya and the Karakoram, it lies athwart two other, the Ladakh range and the Zanskar range. Ladakh is mystical in all the spheres it covers, from nature, geography, sceneries to the modest cultures that it fosters. Right from gompas to the sensational momos, the superabundance of attractions to visit makes this city make it heaven on earth. It is said that only in Ladakh can a man sitting in the sun with his feet in the shade suffer from sunstroke and frostbite at the same time.
Weather:0 C
Ideal duration:5-7 days
Best time:Jun-Sep
Nearest Airport:Leh


Manali,Himachal Pradesh

"Lover's Paradise - India's Honeymoon capital"
Manali is also home to a tiny slice of history, in the form of the Naggar castle. Located among the breathtaking forests in Naggar town, the Naggar Castle is a stunning historical edifice. Once used as the residence of Raja Sidh Singh of Kullu, the castle is a fine blend of traditional Himalayan and European architecture. With majestic fireplaces, beautifully built staircases, and meticulous wood and stone works, the Naggar Castle is a must-visit when you're in Manali.
Weather:9 C
Ideal duration:2-4 days
Best time:Oct-Jun
Nearest Airport:Chandigarh


Nainital,Uttarakhand

"Misty lakes and mountains"
The gem of Uttarakhand - Nainital is a charming hill station that sits prettily at the green foothills of the Kumaon ranges in the Himalayas. Located at an elevation of around 1938 metres, the epicentre of the town's popularity and beauty lies within the gorgeous Naini Lake, after which the town is named. Founded by the British due to its resemblance to the Cumbrian Lake district, Nainital brims with elegant colonial structures that amplify the beauty of this place. The Naini Lake, along with the other lakes in the area, has earned this town the title of 'City of Lakes'.
Weather:25 C
Ideal duration:2-3 days
Best time:Throughout the year
Nearest Airport:Dehradun


Mcleodganj,Himachal Pradesh

"Land of His Holiness the Dalai Lama - Little Lhasa"
Also known as Little Lhasa and famous around the world for being home to the Tibetan spiritual leader Dalai Lama, Mcleodganj is a beautiful town situated near upper Dharamsala. Nestled amidst majestic hills and lush greenery, this town is culturally blessed by a prominent Tibetan influence owing to the major settlement of Tibetans here. Mcleodganj has one of the most mesmerising landscapes in the entire state of Himachal Pradesh and attracts a lot of tourists throughout the year. The towns of Dharamsala, Mcleodganj, Bhagsu Nag and Kangra are situated very close to each other and tourists must cover all these destinations while travelling here.
Weather:26 C
Ideal duration: 2-3 days
Best time:October - June
Nearest Airport:Jammu
